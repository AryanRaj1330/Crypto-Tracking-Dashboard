Chain Link

Chainlink, introduced in 2017, is a decentralized oracle network that connects blockchain smart contracts to real-world data, enabling secure and accurate off-chain interactions. Its native token, LINK, incentivizes data providers and maintains network integrity. By providing reliable data for decentralized finance (DeFi), insurance, gaming, and more, Chainlink enhances blockchain functionality and scalability. It employs a tamper-proof, decentralized system of oracles to ensure data accuracy and trustworthiness. As a critical infrastructure layer, Chainlink drives innovation by bridging the gap between blockchain technology and real-world applications securely and efficiently.
