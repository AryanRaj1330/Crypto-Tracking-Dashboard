Stellar Lumens

Stellar Lumens (XLM), created in 2014 by Jed McCaleb, is a blockchain platform focused on enabling fast, low-cost cross-border payments and financial inclusion. Stellar facilitates seamless transactions between fiat currencies and digital assets, making it ideal for remittances and microtransactions. Its native token, Lumens (XLM), acts as a bridge currency to reduce transaction costs and prevent network spam. With a decentralized, energy-efficient network, Stellar emphasizes scalability and interoperability. By partnering with financial institutions, Stellar aims to build a global payment network, empowering individuals and businesses in underserved regions.
