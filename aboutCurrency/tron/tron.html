Tron

Tron (TRX), launched in 2017 by Justin Sun, is a blockchain platform designed to decentralize the internet by enabling creators to publish, store, and monetize content directly without intermediaries. Tron focuses on high scalability, offering fast and cost-efficient transactions with its delegated proof-of-stake (DPoS) consensus mechanism. Its ecosystem supports decentralized applications (dApps), smart contracts, and token creation, empowering developers and users alike. Tron's native cryptocurrency, TRX, fuels network operations and incentivizes participation. With partnerships and acquisitions, including BitTorrent, Tron aims to transform the digital content industry by promoting decentralization and empowering creators worldwide.
