Binance Coin

Binance, founded in 2017 by Changpeng Zhao, is one of the world's largest cryptocurrency exchanges, offering a platform for trading a wide range of digital assets. Renowned for its high-speed transactions and user-friendly interface, Binance supports spot, futures, and margin trading. It has its native cryptocurrency, Binance Coin (BNB), which powers its ecosystem, offering reduced trading fees and other benefits. Binance also provides services like staking, lending, and a decentralized exchange (DEX). Despite facing regulatory scrutiny, Binance continues to lead the crypto market by expanding its offerings, enabling millions worldwide to access cryptocurrencies and participate in decentralized finance.
