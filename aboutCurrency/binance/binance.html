Binance Coin

Binance Coin (BNB) is a cryptocurrency launched by the Binance exchange, one of the world's largest cryptocurrency trading platforms. Initially created on the Ethereum blockchain, BNB later migrated to Binance's native blockchain, Binance Chain. It was designed primarily as a utility token to facilitate transactions and provide users with discounts on trading fees within the Binance ecosystem. Over time, BNB has expanded its use cases, including payment for goods and services, participation in token sales on the Binance Launchpad, and powering the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), which supports decentralized applications and smart contracts. Binance conducts regular coin burns, reducing the total supply of BNB to increase its value and maintain scarcity. With its growing ecosystem, BNB has gained significant traction among investors and developers, cementing its position as one of the most prominent cryptocurrencies in the market.
